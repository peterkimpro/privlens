Privlens Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Privlens analyze my documents? Privlens uses Apple Foundation Models, an AI that runs entirely on your iPhone. Your documents are never sent to any server or cloud service.
Why did the AI skip analysis on my document? Apple's on-device AI has built-in safety filters that occasionally flag benign content. Try scanning the document again — the app automatically retries with different approaches.
Can I copy text from my scanned documents? Yes. Go to the document detail view, tap the "Raw Text" tab, and use the "Copy All" button or select any portion of text.
How do I rename a document? Open the document, then tap the pencil icon in the top toolbar to enter a new name.
What happens to my documents if I delete the app? All scanned documents and analysis results are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data is permanently removed.
Is the app really free? Yes. All features are free with no limits. There is an optional tip jar in Settings if you'd like to support development.
What iPhone do I need? Privlens requires an iPhone running iOS 26 or later with Apple Foundation Models support.
The analysis says "Unknown" as the document type. Is that a problem? No. The AI analyzes the content regardless of type classification. You can rename the document to anything you'd like using the pencil icon.
