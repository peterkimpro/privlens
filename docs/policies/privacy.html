Privlens Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

The short version: Privlens does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device. Period.

What Data We Collect

None. Privlens does not collect any personal data, usage data, analytics, or telemetry of any kind.

Document Processing

All document scanning, OCR text extraction, and AI analysis happens entirely on your iPhone using Apple's on-device technologies (VisionKit and Apple Foundation Models). Your documents are never sent to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Data Storage

Scanned documents and analysis results are stored locally on your device in the app's private sandbox. This data is not accessible to other apps and is removed when you delete the app.

Network Connections

Privlens makes zero network connections for document processing. The only network activity occurs if you choose to:

  • Make a tip purchase (processed by Apple's App Store)
  • Share an analysis result using the iOS share sheet

Third-Party Services

Privlens does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data collection services.

Apple App Store Analytics

Apple may collect aggregated, anonymized analytics about app usage (downloads, sessions, crashes) through App Store Connect. This is standard for all iOS apps and is governed by Apple's own privacy policy. We do not have access to any individual user data through this.

Children's Privacy

Privlens does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require an account or any personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update this page and the "Last updated" date above. Given our commitment to privacy, any changes will only ever maintain or strengthen these protections.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@privlens.app.
